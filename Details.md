Date: 21 May 2026

Duration: 60 Minutes
Objective Type Assessment
Cyber Security & General Aptitude Based Questions

The examination tested analytical thinking, cybersecurity awareness, and problem-solving skills under time pressure.

Time management and focused thinking are essential skills in cybersecurity.
